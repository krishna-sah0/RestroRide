Frequently Asked Questions

Is it legal to sell subscription access?

This depends on the terms of service of each platform. We recommend checking the specific service's terms before listing. SubSwap is a platform for connecting users and doesn't guarantee the legality of all transactions.

How do I ensure safe transactions?

Always verify the seller's identity, use secure payment methods, and never share personal passwords. Consider using escrow services for high-value transactions.

What if the subscription doesn't work?

Contact the seller first to resolve any issues. If problems persist, report the issue to our support team for assistance.

Can I sell any type of subscription?

We support most digital subscriptions including streaming services, software, and online services. Physical memberships may have location restrictions.
